FootyFeverz Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 10, 2025

What we collect

  • Account info: email, username.
  • User-generated content: photos/videos you upload, comments, likes.
  • Usage data: app interactions, device info (OS, model), crash/analytics data.

How we use it

  • To provide the app (store your uploads, enable comments/likes, show content).
  • To secure your account and prevent abuse.
  • To improve performance and fix bugs.

Sharing

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We share data with service providers only to operate the app (e.g., Firebase for auth, storage, database; analytics/crash reporting).

Your choices

  • You can request deletion of your account and content by emailing us.
  • You can stop using the app and request data removal at any time.

Data storage

  • Data is stored in Firebase (authentication, Firestore, Storage).
